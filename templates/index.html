Who we are?

We are Hope Refugee Settlement Services International, a nonprofit organization that is committed to providing support to refugees and other vulnerable migrants with the process of settling in Canada, and life after settlement.

Our goal is to preserve, strengthen, and promote individual well-being and family ties.

Services we Offer to Refugees

Here are some of the services that HRSSI Hope offers
Eritreans in Canada and Abroad.

Refugee Sponsorship

We protect refugees in Canada and overseas and alleviate barriers to family reunification and delays in processing of privately sponsored refugees.

Legal Aid

We provide legal aid and support for refugees. This includes finding you a legal representative and translation services.

Housing & Jobs

For newly landed and early stage refugees in Canada, we help them with finding appropriate housing and employment for their situations.

We need your help

HRSSI was founded by a group of Eritrean former refugees who understand the challenged faced by other refugees when going through the process of immigration to a foreign land.

We know the difficulties refugees face when first planning to immigrate and when they arrive. Help us provide them all the necessary assitance and information needed to ensure thier successful integration into Canada.
